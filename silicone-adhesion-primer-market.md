<p><p>The silicone adhesion primer market is categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based primers are eco-friendly, low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and offer easy cleanup, making them suitable for indoor applications. They provide good adhesion on various substrates. Conversely, solvent-based primers offer robust performance in harsh conditions and are preferred for exterior applications, providing enhanced durability and adhesion. Each type has unique advantages, catering to different project requirements and environmental considerations, thereby influencing customer choice.</p></p>
