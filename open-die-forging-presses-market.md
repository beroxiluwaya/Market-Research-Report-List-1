<p><p>Open die forging presses are essential in metal forming, classified into several types. Push-down forging presses apply force by pressing the die down onto the workpiece, ensuring precision in forming. Pull-down forging presses, conversely, pull the die downward to shape the metal, offering control and flexibility. Other types may include hybrid systems or specialized configurations tailored for unique applications. The choice of press depends on the material properties and desired outcomes in the forging process.</p></p>
