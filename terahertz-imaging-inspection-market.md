<p><strong>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging Inspection market comprises two primary types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging detects naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects, providing insights based on thermal differences. It is commonly used for surveillance and security applications. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the use of external terahertz sources to illuminate objects, allowing for more detailed analysis, such as material characterization and detection of concealed items. Both methods enhance non-destructive testing and evaluation capabilities across various industries.</p></p>
