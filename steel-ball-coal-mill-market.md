<p><p>The steel ball coal mill market is categorized into three main types based on operational speed. Low-speed coal mills operate at less than 50 RPM, making them suitable for grinding and drying coal. Medium-speed coal mills function between 50 to 300 RPM and are commonly used in thermal power plants due to their efficiency. High-speed coal mills, operating above 300 RPM, provide rapid grinding but may generate more noise and wear, making them less prevalent in large-scale applications.</p></p>
