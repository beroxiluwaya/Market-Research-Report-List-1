<p><ul><li>Thermoset Composites</li><li>Thermoplastic Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass fiber complex materials market encompasses two main types: thermoset composites and thermoplastic composites. Thermoset composites are made from resin systems that harden permanently when cured, offering high strength and thermal resistance, making them suitable for applications in aerospace and automotive sectors. Conversely, thermoplastic composites utilize resins that can be repeatedly melted and reshaped, allowing for easier processing and recycling. These materials are favored in industries seeking lightweight, durable solutions, enhancing performance and sustainability in various applications.</p></p>
