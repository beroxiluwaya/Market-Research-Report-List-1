<p><p>The digital receptacle tester market includes various types, notably GFCI (Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter) and AFCI (Arc Fault Circuit Interrupter) testers. GFCI testers are designed to check for ground faults, ensuring electrical safety in damp locations by preventing electrical shock. On the other hand, AFCI testers detect arc faults, which can lead to electrical fires, and are crucial for safeguarding residential circuits. Both testers enhance safety measures in electrical installations, catering to the increasing demand for reliable electrical diagnostics.</p></p>
