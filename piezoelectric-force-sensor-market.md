<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Force Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm structure</li><li>Pistona structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric force sensor market is mainly categorized into two types: diaphragm structure and piston structure. </p><p>Diaphragm structure sensors use a thin diaphragm that deforms under applied force, generating an electrical charge proportional to the force. They are known for their compactness and versatility in various applications. </p><p>Piston structure sensors, on the other hand, utilize a piston mechanism that applies force to a piezoelectric element, providing high sensitivity and robust performance, typically suited for industrial and heavy-load applications.</p></p>
