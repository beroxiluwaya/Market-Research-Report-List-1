<p><strong>The Post CMP Cleaning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acid Material</li><li>Alkaline Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Post CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) cleaning involves the removal of residues after the polishing process in semiconductor manufacturing. The market is divided into two main types: acid materials and alkaline materials. Acid materials, such as sulfuric acid or nitric acid, effectively dissolve metal oxides and residues. Alkaline materials, like ammonium hydroxide, are used to neutralize acidic residues and remove organic contaminants. Both types are essential for ensuring the cleanliness and integrity of semiconductor wafers before further processing.</p></p>
