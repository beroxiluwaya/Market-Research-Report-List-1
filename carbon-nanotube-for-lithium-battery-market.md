<p><p>Carbon nanotubes (CNTs) are essential in the lithium battery market, enhancing performance and conductivity. Single-walled nanotubes (SWNTs) consist of a single graphene cylinder, offering superior electrical properties and light weight, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Conversely, multi-walled nanotubes (MWNTs) contain multiple concentric cylinders, providing increased mechanical strength and thermal stability. Both types improve energy density, charging rates, and overall cycle life in lithium batteries, driving advancements in energy storage technologies for various applications.</p></p>
