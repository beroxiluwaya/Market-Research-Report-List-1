Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring unidirectional flow in pipelines. Ball valves provide reliable on/off control with minimal pressure drop, ideal for harsh environments. Butterfly valves are used for throttling and flow regulation in large pipelines due to their lightweight and compact design.
