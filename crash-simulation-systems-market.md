<p><ul><li>Side Impact Simulation</li><li>Vehicle Pitch Simulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crash simulation systems encompass various market types, including side impact simulation and vehicle pitch simulation. Side impact simulation focuses on analyzing the vehicle's response during a lateral collision, evaluating structural integrity and occupant safety. Vehicle pitch simulation, on the other hand, examines how a vehicle reacts during braking and acceleration, assessing the dynamics involved in forward and backward motion. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle design, ensuring compliance with safety regulations, and improving overall crashworthiness.</p></p>
