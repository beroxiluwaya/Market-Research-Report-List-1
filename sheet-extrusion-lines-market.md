<p><strong>The Sheet Extrusion Lines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sheet extrusion lines can be categorized into two main market types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical sheet extrusion lines are designed to operate with the die positioned above the cooling system, allowing for gravity-assisted flow and effective cooling, making them suitable for specific applications with height limitations. Conversely, horizontal sheet extrusion lines feature a die positioned parallel to the ground, optimizing material distribution and allowing for larger production capacities, often used in applications requiring extensive sheet widths and thicknesses.</p></p>
