<p><p>The Whole Body Cryo market consists of three primary types: Liquid Nitrogen Therapy, Dry Ice Therapy, and Electric Cryotherapy. </p><p>Liquid Nitrogen Therapy uses extremely cold nitrogen gas to rapidly lower skin temperature, often used in recovery and wellness settings. </p><p>Dry Ice Therapy involves the application of solid carbon dioxide, providing a more controlled cooling effect. </p><p>Electric Cryotherapy employs electric machines to simulate cold exposure through cooling panels, offering a convenient and consistent method for therapeutic purposes. Each type serves various recovery and therapeutic needs.</p></p>
