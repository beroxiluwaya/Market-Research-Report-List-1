<p><strong>The Hepatitis E Diagnostic Tests Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ELISA HEV IgM Test kits</li><li>ELISA HEV IgG Test Kits</li><li>RT-PCR Test Kits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hepatitis E diagnostic tests market comprises three primary types: ELISA HEV IgM test kits, ELISA HEV IgG test kits, and RT-PCR test kits. ELISA HEV IgM kits detect recent infections by identifying IgM antibodies, indicating acute hepatitis E. ELISA HEV IgG kits, on the other hand, identify IgG antibodies to indicate past infections or immunity. RT-PCR test kits utilize real-time polymerase chain reaction technology to detect HEV RNA, providing rapid diagnosis and confirming active infections, essential for effective management and treatment.</p></p>
