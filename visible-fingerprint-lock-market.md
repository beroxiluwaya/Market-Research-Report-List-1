<p><p>The Visible Fingerprint Lock market comprises two primary types: Optical Fingerprint Locks and Semiconductor Fingerprint Locks. Optical Fingerprint Locks utilize light to capture fingerprints, creating a detailed image for authentication, making them effective in well-lit conditions. In contrast, Semiconductor Fingerprint Locks rely on semiconductor sensors that read the unique patterns of a fingerprint through electrical signals, offering enhanced durability and performance in varied lighting. Both technologies provide secure access control solutions, catering to different user preferences and security needs.</p></p>
