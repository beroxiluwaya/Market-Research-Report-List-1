<p><p>Queue kiosks are categorized into three main types based on their installation style: floor-standing, wall-mounted, and countertop. Floor-standing kiosks are typically large, standalone units ideal for high-traffic areas, providing easy accessibility. Wall-mounted kiosks save space and are suitable for environments with limited floor area, conveniently allowing users to interact without obstruction. Countertop kiosks are compact and perfect for service counters, offering a user-friendly interface while maximizing limited space. Each type is designed to enhance customer experience and streamline queue management.</p></p>
