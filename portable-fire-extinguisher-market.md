<p><p>The portable fire extinguisher market comprises several types, each designed for specific fire scenarios. The dry powder type is effective against Class A, B, and C fires, using a non-toxic powder to smother flames. Foam extinguishers are ideal for flammable liquid fires, creating a blanket to suppress vapor release. Carbon dioxide extinguishers are best suited for electrical fires, displacing oxygen while leaving no residue. Each type serves a unique purpose, ensuring effective fire suppression across various environments.</p></p>
