<p><p>The hot swap controllers market is segmented into high voltage, low voltage, and PCI hot swap controllers, each catering to specific applications. High voltage hot swap controllers manage power supply for devices operating at elevated voltages, ensuring safe insertion and removal. Low voltage hot swap controllers handle standard voltage levels, facilitating seamless component swapping in consumer electronics. PCI hot swap controllers are designed for server and data center environments, allowing the hot swapping of PCI cards without disrupting system functionality, enhancing uptime and reliability.</p></p>
