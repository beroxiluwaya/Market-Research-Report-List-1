<p><ul><li>Reaction Torque Sensor</li><li>Rotary Torque Sensor</li><li>Super Acoustic Wave (SAW) Torque Sensor</li><li>Optical Torque Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The torque sensor market comprises various types catering to different applications. Reaction torque sensors measure static torque by detecting the forces exerted on a fixed object. Rotary torque sensors assess torque in rotating systems through a rotational measurement. Super Acoustic Wave (SAW) torque sensors utilize acoustic waves for precise torque measurements. Optical torque sensors employ light-based technology for non-contact measurement. Other types may include strain gauge and magnetostrictive sensors, offering diverse options for industries requiring accurate torque monitoring.</p></p>
