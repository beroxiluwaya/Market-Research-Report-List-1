<p><strong>The Windsurf Masts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SDM Masts</li><li>RDM Masts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The windsurf mast market features two primary types: SDM (Standard Diameter Mast) and RDM (Reduced Diameter Mast). SDM masts are thicker and provide increased stiffness, making them ideal for high-performance sailing and speed. In contrast, RDM masts are thinner and more flexible, offering better control and maneuverability, particularly in lighter winds or for freestyle tricks. Both types cater to different sailing styles and preferences, influencing performance and overall experience on the water.</p></p>
