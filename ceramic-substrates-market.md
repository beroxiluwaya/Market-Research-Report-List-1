<p><p>The ceramic substrates market comprises several key types: alumina, aluminum nitride, silicon nitride, and beryllium oxide, each offering unique properties for electronics and thermal management. Alumina is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and electrical insulation. Aluminum nitride excels in thermal conductivity and is ideal for high-power applications. Silicon nitride offers superior mechanical strength and thermal stability, suitable for advanced applications. Beryllium oxide provides exceptional thermal conductivity but poses health risks, limiting its widespread use.</p></p>
