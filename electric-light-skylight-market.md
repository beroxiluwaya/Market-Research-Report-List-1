<p><p>The Electric Light Skylight market encompasses two primary types: pitched roof and flat roof skylights. Pitched roof skylights are designed for sloped roofing, allowing for effective water drainage and enhancing natural light in spaces like attics and vaulted ceilings. Flat roof skylights, on the other hand, are suited for horizontal surfaces, offering a sleek aesthetic while providing natural illumination. Both types can integrate electric lighting and automated features, enhancing their functionality and boosting energy efficiency in residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
