<p><p>The High Voltage Reed Switch market is categorized into three main types based on their contact configurations. Form A switches feature normally open contacts that close when a magnetic field is applied, suitable for applications needing simple on/off control. Form B switches have normally closed contacts that open in the presence of a magnetic field, ideal for safety applications. Form C switches offer both normally open and normally closed contacts, providing versatility for complex circuitry and various signal control requirements.</p></p>
