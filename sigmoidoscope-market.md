<p><p>The sigmoidoscope market is primarily divided into two types: flexible sigmoidoscopes and rigid sigmoidoscopes. Flexible sigmoidoscopes are designed with a bendable body, allowing for easier navigation through the colon, providing enhanced comfort and visualization during procedures. Rigid sigmoidoscopes, on the other hand, have a straight structure and are typically used for more straightforward examinations but may result in more discomfort for patients. Both types serve essential roles in colorectal health diagnostics, influencing their market demand and applications in healthcare.</p></p>
