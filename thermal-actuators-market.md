<p><p>The thermal actuators market can be categorized into three primary types: squeeze type, diaphragm type, and piston type. Squeeze type actuators use thermal expansion to create linear motion by compressing a flexible material. Diaphragm type actuators utilize temperature variations to deform a diaphragm, controlling fluid flow. Piston type actuators employ thermal energy to move a piston within a cylinder, enabling precise movement and actuation. Each type serves specific applications across industries, including automation, HVAC, and automotive systems.</p></p>
