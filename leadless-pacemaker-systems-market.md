<p><p>Leadless pacemaker systems are innovative cardiac devices that eliminate the need for traditional leads, offering less invasive procedures. Single-chambered systems are designed to stimulate either the right atrium or right ventricle, making them suitable for patients with specific rhythm disorders. In contrast, double-chambered systems connect to both chambers, allowing for more comprehensive heart rate management. These advancements enhance patient comfort, reduce risks of lead-related complications, and broaden treatment options for various arrhythmias, propelling growth in the market.</p></p>
