<p><p>Fibreboard for construction is categorized into three main types based on density. Low Density Fiberboard (LDF) is lightweight and primarily used for insulation and decorative applications. Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF) offers a balance of strength and versatility, making it ideal for furniture, cabinetry, and moldings. High Density Fiberboard (HDF) is robust and dense, suitable for flooring and structural components, providing enhanced durability and resistance to wear. Each type serves specific purposes in construction, catering to diverse project needs.</p></p>
