<p><strong>The 3D Automotive Camera Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS)</li><li>Charge-Coupled Device (CCD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Automotive Camera Module market features primarily two sensor types: Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) and Charge-Coupled Device (CCD). CMOS sensors are widely favored for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and ability to integrate additional functions on a single chip, making them ideal for real-time applications. In contrast, CCD sensors deliver superior image quality and low noise levels, which are essential for high-resolution imaging in complex driving environments. Each type serves distinct automotive needs, enhancing safety and automation.</p></p>
