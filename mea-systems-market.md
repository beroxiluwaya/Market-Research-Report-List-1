<p><ul><li>Used in Vivo</li><li>Used in Vitro</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEA (Multi-Electrode Array) systems are utilized in two primary market types: in vivo and in vitro. In vivo MEA systems are deployed for live animal testing, enabling researchers to monitor neurological activity and drug effects in real-time within a living organism. In contrast, in vitro MEA systems are used in controlled laboratory settings, often with cell cultures or tissue samples, to study cellular behavior and interactions in a highly controlled environment. Both applications facilitate advancements in neuroscience and pharmacology.</p></p>
