<p><p>The automotive axial leaded multilayer ceramic capacitor market consists primarily of two types: X7R and Z5U. X7R capacitors exhibit stable capacitance over a wide temperature range, making them suitable for automotive applications requiring reliability under varying conditions. In contrast, Z5U capacitors offer higher dielectric constants but have less stability and a wider capacitance variation with temperature and voltage, making them more suited for less critical applications. Both types contribute to the growing demand for compact and efficient electronic components in the automotive industry.</p></p>
