<p><p>The coated abrasives market includes sanding blocks, pads, sheets, and discs, each designed for specific applications. Sanding blocks are handheld tools with abrasive surfaces, ideal for flat or contoured surfaces. Sanding pads are flexible, often used with power tools for smooth finishes on uneven surfaces. Sheets are versatile, cuttable abrasives for manual or machine use. Sanding discs are circular abrasives attached to power tools, suitable for rapid material removal and polishing on flat or curved surfaces. All cater to woodworking, metalworking, and finishing tasks.</p></p>
