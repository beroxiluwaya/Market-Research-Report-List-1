<p><p>Seismic base isolation systems enhance the resilience of structures against earthquakes by decoupling the building from ground motion. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Natural Rubber Bearings (NRB)**, which use natural rubber to absorb seismic energy.</p><p>2. **Lead Rubber Bearings (LRB)**, incorporating lead cores to provide damping and enhance energy dissipation.</p><p>3. **High-Damping Rubber Bearings (HDR)**, designed to offer improved damping properties for better performance during seismic events. Together, these systems provide tailored solutions for varying structural needs and seismic challenges.</p></p>
