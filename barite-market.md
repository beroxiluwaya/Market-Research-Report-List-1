<p><p>The barite market is categorized based on specific gravity, which ranges from 3.9g/cm³ to 4.3g/cm³. Each type serves different industrial applications, particularly in oil and gas drilling, where higher density provides better weight for wellbore stability. The 3.9g/cm³ type is often used for lighter applications, while the 4.0g/cm³ and 4.1g/cm³ grades are common in drilling fluids. The 4.2g/cm³ and 4.3g/cm³ variants are preferred for more demanding environments, offering enhanced performance in extreme conditions.</p></p>
