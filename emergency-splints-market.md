<p><p>The emergency splints market is categorized into three main types: rigid, semi-rigid, and malleable emergency splints. Rigid emergency splints are designed to immobilize fractures and provide maximum support, ideal for serious injuries. Semi-rigid splints offer flexibility, allowing for some movement while still providing support, suitable for less severe injuries. Malleable emergency splints are versatile, easily shaped to conform to various body parts, making them adaptable for a range of emergency situations. Each type addresses different injury requirements in emergency care.</p></p>
