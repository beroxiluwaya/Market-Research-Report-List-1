<p><p>The global top adhesives market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into one-component epoxies, two-component epoxies, and UV curing systems. One-component epoxies are ready to use, requiring no mixing and ideal for quick applications. Two-component epoxies involve a curing agent, offering greater strength and versatility but necessitating precise mixing. UV curing systems utilize ultraviolet light to initiate rapid curing, providing fast processing times and increased production efficiency, making them suitable for industries like electronics and automotive.</p></p>
