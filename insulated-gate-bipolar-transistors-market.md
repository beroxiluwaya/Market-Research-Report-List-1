<p><p>The insulated-gate bipolar transistor (IGBT) market is primarily divided into two types: discrete IGBTs and IGBT modules. Discrete IGBTs are single devices used in various applications for efficient power control and switching, catering to smaller-scale needs like consumer electronics. In contrast, IGBT modules comprise multiple IGBT chips integrated into a single package, offering higher power handling and thermal performance. These modules are ideal for industrial applications, renewable energy systems, and electric vehicles, where robust performance and efficiency are crucial.</p></p>
