In addition, ongoing developments in bioinformatics and data analysis tools are enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of WES.</p><p>Emerging trends include the integration of WES with other genomic technologies, such as whole-genome sequencing and transcriptomics, which provide a more comprehensive understanding of genetic variations.
