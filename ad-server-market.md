<p><p>The ad server market is primarily categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ad servers operate on the internet, allowing users to access their functionality from any location, ensuring scalability and ease of integration with existing systems. Conversely, on-premises ad servers are installed directly on the user's infrastructure, offering greater control over data and security but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Both types cater to different business needs, influencing marketing strategies and operational efficiency.</p></p>
