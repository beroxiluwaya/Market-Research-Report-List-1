Horizontal balers, on the other hand, accommodate larger volumes and offer high throughput by horizontally feeding materials, making them suitable for industrial applications. The "others" category includes specialized or hybrid balers that combine features of both types, addressing diverse operational needs and providing flexibility for various waste management scenarios.</p></p>
