<p><p>The furniture drawer system market is divided into two main types: single-walled and double-walled drawers. Single-walled drawers are constructed from a single layer of material, providing a lightweight and cost-effective solution for basic storage needs. The choice between these types often depends on factors like intended use, required strength, and budget considerations.</p></p>
