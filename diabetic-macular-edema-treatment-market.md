<p><p>The Diabetic Macular Edema (DME) treatment market includes several types of interventions. Laser Photocoagulation Therapy involves focused laser beams to reduce fluid leakage and prevent vision loss. Pharmacological Therapy comprises anti-VEGF agents like Bevacizumab and Ranibizumab, which inhibit vascular growth to stabilize vision. Additionally, Triamcinolone, a corticosteroid, is used to decrease inflammation and fluid accumulation. Each treatment type aims to manage DME effectively and preserve visual acuity in patients with diabetes-related retinal complications.</p></p>
