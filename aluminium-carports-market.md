<p><p>The aluminum carport market primarily features two types: vertical roof carports and regular roof carports. Vertical roof carports are designed with a sloped roof that allows for better water drainage and snow shedding, making them ideal for areas with heavy precipitation. In contrast, regular roof carports have a horizontal design, offering a more economical option, but may perform less effectively in severe weather. Both types provide protection for vehicles while enhancing aesthetics and durability.</p></p>
