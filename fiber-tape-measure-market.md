<p><p>The fiber tape measure market is divided into two main types: pocket tapes and surveyors' tapes. Pocket tapes are compact, portable measuring tools designed for general use, suitable for everyday tasks like DIY projects or home improvement. They are typically made from flexible materials for ease of handling. Surveyors' tapes, on the other hand, are longer and sturdier, intended for precise measurements over large distances, making them essential in construction, engineering, and surveying applications. Each type serves distinct user needs.</p></p>
