<p><p>The ethylene scavenger market is categorized into several types based on the materials used to absorb or neutralize ethylene gas, which accelerates fruit ripening. Potassium Permanganate (KMnO4) is a chemical compound that effectively absorbs ethylene, while zeolite is a mineral-based adsorbent with high porosity. Activated carbon is another efficient adsorbent used for its high surface area. Pumice, a porous volcanic rock, also serves as a natural alternative. Other scavengers may include organic compounds or blended materials tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
