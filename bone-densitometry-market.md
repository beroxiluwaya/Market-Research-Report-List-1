<p><ul><li>X-Ray Bone Densitometry</li><li>Ultrasonic Bone Densitometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone densitometry market is segmented into X-Ray Bone Densitometry and Ultrasonic Bone Densitometry. X-Ray Bone Densitometry, commonly known as Dual-Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA), uses low-dose X-rays to measure bone mineral density, offering precise results and is widely used in clinical settings. In contrast, Ultrasonic Bone Densitometry utilizes ultrasound waves to assess bone density, providing a radiation-free alternative. This method is portable and often used in screening and pediatric applications, appealing for its ease of use and safety profile.</p></p>
