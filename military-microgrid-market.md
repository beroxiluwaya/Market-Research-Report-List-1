<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied Type Microgrid</li><li>Independent Type Microgrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Military Microgrid market includes two primary types: Grid-Tied and Independent Microgrids. Grid-Tied Microgrids are connected to the main electrical grid, allowing for energy exchange and backup capabilities during emergencies. They enhance energy resilience while enabling integration of renewable sources. In contrast, Independent Microgrids operate autonomously, providing critical power supply in remote or hostile environments without reliance on external grids. Independent systems are essential for mission continuity, ensuring operational readiness regardless of external conditions or disruptions.</p></p>
