<p><strong>The Bottle Cap Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Rotary"</li><li>"In-line"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bottle cap machine market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and in-line systems. Rotary machines use a circular arrangement that allows for simultaneous processing of multiple bottles, enhancing speed and efficiency, ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, in-line machines operate in a straight line, where bottles move sequentially through each operation. This type is often more straightforward to integrate into existing production lines and is suitable for diverse bottle sizes and cap types, ensuring flexibility in manufacturing.</p></p>
