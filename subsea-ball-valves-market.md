<p><p>The subsea ball valves market comprises primarily two types: 2-way valves and 3-way valves. 2-way valves are designed to control the flow of fluids in a single direction, effectively isolating or regulating flow in pipelines. In contrast, 3-way valves facilitate multiple flow paths, allowing for the diversion or mixing of fluids. These valves are crucial for subsea applications, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability in oil and gas production while maintaining safety and integrity under high-pressure conditions.</p></p>
