<p><p>The high-pressure seals market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. Metal seals are known for their durability in extreme conditions, while thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) is valued for its flexibility and abrasion resistance. Hydrogenated nitrile butadiene rubber (HNBR) offers excellent chemical resistance, making it ideal for oil and gas applications. Fluoroelastomers provide superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, suitable for harsh environments. Ethylene propylene diene monomer (EPDM) seals are preferred for their weather resistance and elasticity, often used in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
