<p><p>The liquid packaging market is primarily categorized into flexible and rigid liquid packaging. Flexible liquid packaging involves materials like pouches and bags that can conform to the shape of the liquid, providing lightweight and space-efficient options. This type is ideal for products like beverages and sauces. Rigid liquid packaging, on the other hand, includes bottles, jars, and containers that offer sturdy protection and are often used for products requiring longer shelf life or enhanced durability. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
