<p><p>The Indoor Food Delivery Robot market can be categorized into two primary types: Wireless Type and Track Type. Wireless Type robots navigate using advanced sensors and mapping technology, allowing them to move freely within indoor spaces and adapt to changing environments. In contrast, Track Type robots operate along predefined paths, relying on physical tracks or guide lines, which provides a stable delivery route but limits flexibility. Both types enhance efficiency in food delivery within restaurants, hotels, and large facilities.</p></p>
