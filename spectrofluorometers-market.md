<p><p>The spectrofluorometers market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Fluorescence lifetime measurement instruments assess the time it takes for a fluorescent molecule to return to its ground state, providing insights into molecular dynamics. Time-integration spectrofluorometers capture fluorescence over defined periods, enhancing sensitivity. Compact spectrofluorometers are portable for field use, while NIR spectrofluorometers focus on near-infrared fluorescence analysis, suitable for biological and material studies. Static spectrofluorometers provide straightforward measurements of fluorescence without time-dependent analysis. Each type serves distinct scientific and industrial needs.</p></p>
