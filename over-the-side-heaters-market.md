<p><p>The Over the Side Heaters market features two primary types: L-Shaped and O-Shaped Over-the-Side Immersion Heaters. L-Shaped Over-the-Side Immersion Heaters are designed for vertical installations, providing efficient heating to fluids in tanks or containers. Conversely, O-Shaped Over-the-Side Immersion Heaters, which resemble a loop, distribute heat evenly around the tank, ensuring uniform temperature maintenance. Both types cater to various industrial applications, improving process efficiency by delivering consistent heat in demanding operational environments.</p></p>
