Fine aluminium alloy powders are used in additive manufacturing and high-precision applications due to their small particle size, enhancing melt flow and sintering properties. Coarse aluminium alloy powders, with larger particle sizes, are commonly employed in bulk manufacturing processes, providing strength and durability in industrial applications.</p></p>
