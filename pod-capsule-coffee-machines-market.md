<p><p>In the pod and capsule coffee machines market, two main types are identified: closed source systems and open source systems. Closed source systems are proprietary designs that use compatible capsules, often limiting users to specific brands, ensuring consistent quality and convenience. In contrast, open source systems allow for greater flexibility, enabling users to use various coffee types and brands, promoting customization and potentially lower costs. This differentiation caters to diverse consumer preferences for convenience versus versatility in brewing options.</p></p>
