<p><p>The automotive navigation solutions market encompasses various types, including 3D navigation, 2D navigation, and DGPS. </p><p>3D navigation provides realistic, three-dimensional maps that enhance spatial awareness and allow for better route visualization. </p><p>2D navigation, on the other hand, offers flat, two-dimensional maps, focusing on essential directions and distances. </p><p>DGPS (Differential GPS) enhances GPS accuracy by using additional ground-based reference stations, delivering precise location data through auxiliary serial ports, ensuring reliable navigation for automotive applications.</p></p>
